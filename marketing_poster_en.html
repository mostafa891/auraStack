Core Features

  • 🛡️

    B2B Multi-Tenancy & Workspace Isolation

    Full data isolation with BOLA-safe queries, team invitations, and role-based access (Owner / Admin / Member) scoped to subscription limits.

  • 🔐

    SOC2-Grade Security & Auth

    Social login (OAuth), TOTP 2FA, brute-force lockout, CSRF & XSS hardening, secure-cookie sessions, and allauth integration out of the box.

  • 💳

    Subscriptions & Multi-Gateway Billing

    Stripe, Paymob, and PayPal support with HMAC-verified webhooks, plan enforcement, upgrade/downgrade flows, and invoice generation.

  • ⚙️

    Async Task Queue & Job Scheduling

    Django Q2 hybrid worker handles background jobs on DB or Redis, with automatic success-record pruning and retry policies built in.

  • 📝

    Markdown Blog with Unicode Slugs

    A fully-featured content engine with collision-safe slug generation supporting Arabic, CJK, and all Unicode character sets.
